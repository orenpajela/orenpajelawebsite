1/15/2024

The future is exciting.

Or at least, it should be. I've been thinking about what to do in the future, and honestly sometimes I feel like there's a lot of paralysis by analysis. I am very confident in my ability to get and maintain a job- getting laid off was actually a blessing in disguise because I learned that my skills are valued by the market. People want what I have to give, and to know that I have honed my craft to that point is a really good feeling. But at the same time, I think I have to ask myself- What do I want?

I started a company recently and have been able to find some great clients, and I guess I would like to continue growing that. I'm going to continue growing that. But is that all I want to do? No, I don't think it is. I want to do cool stuff, build cool stuff. Build stupid stuff, pointless stuff- if only because it's going to be fun. I want to design for myself and not for other people. I want to design because I like design, and not because I need to do it for work. Ok yes, design is my job, it's my career. But I know it means so much more to me. It's something that in a way, I was born to do. I was designed to design.

So I guess that's what I'll do. I'm going to keep designing. And yes, for other people, and yes to make money. But also for myself. For fun. For silly reasons. Because it doesn't always have to be serious. There's more to life than work. So I guess that's what I'm on a quest to discover more about.

8/17/2023

“When an unclean spirit comes out of a person, it roams through waterless places looking for rest but doesn’t find any. Then it says, ‘I’ll go back to my house that I came from.’ Returning, it finds the house vacant, swept, and put in order. Then it goes and brings with it seven other spirits more evil than itself, and they enter and settle down there. As a result, that person’s last condition is worse than the first. That’s how it will also be with this evil generation.”
Matthew 12:43-45

So I keep a pretty clean place. And by pretty clean, I mean that it’s actually really nice: Things are neat and tidy, there’s a place for almost everything, and there is enough furniture and space and room so that even if I put some of my clothes on the floor, it’s really easy to pick up and clean up and it doesn’t really look that out of place.

I used to have this verse in my mind for quite some time, and in the past I didn’t really understand it. I still really don’t, but I was just so confused and puzzled by this parable, and it was a little frustrating every time I cleaned my place it popped into my head and that confusion was nagging at me. To be honest, I always had an issue because somehow I felt personally attacked because I keep a clean house, and this person kept a clean house, but somehow keeping a clean house backfired on this person, leaving that person in a condition worse than the first. And of course, like almost everyone else, I can relate to having come across many unclean spirits in my life. And I wanted to keep a clean place but I also don’t want more evil spirits to be hanging out anywhere near me, and so it was just a little bit of an issue.

But this morning, as I was cleaning my place, I was saying a prayer and a tiny bit of understanding came to me. Obviously, I’m at a really low point in my life. Lowest of the low, lower than all of the previous lows, a new record of lows. It’s almost like my condition was worse than before. Well, not almost, it’s exactly like my condition was worse than before. And it really sucks. And I was praying and asking God for help, because I’m struggling every day, and I need to pray just to get through a single day.

Well as I was cleaning, I was asking for the Holy Spirit to fill this place. And that’s when I realized something: the evil spirits were only able to come inside because the house was vacant. If the house was already inhabited by the Holy Spirit, then it would be impossible for the unclean spirit to come back and settle there. In fact, even if at first the evil spirit found the house vacant and goes to find its evil spirit buddies, if when it comes back and the Holy Spirit is there, well those eight evil spirit buddies would be out of luck because they can’t enter.

So I believe that God led me to understand that if I’m going to clean my place, if I’m going to sweep it and put my things into order and have it look neat and tidy, I should do it to invite Him and the Holy Spirit into my house. And beyond that, I should clean it so that I can invite real people into my home so that they can feel love, joy, and peace in this place. Of course, there’s definitely more to this verse that I don’t understand, and I believe that theoretically every single verse in the Bible has an infinite amount of ways to be understood by people, but today I’m thankful for this tiny bit of understanding.

God, thank you for this day that you have made. Thank you for your invention of a home. I pray that you fill my house with your Spirit, and I invite you to reside in my place so that you may be here with me. I pray for a future house on this earth, and I already ask that you go ahead of me to prepare it so that it’s ready to be filled with good memories, good people, and to be a tool to serve you and to build your kingdom. And I thank you for the eternal home that you already have built for us in Heaven. Amen.
